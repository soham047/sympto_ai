Symptom Checker

Convert your current symptoms into a ranked differential of three most probable conditions, accompanied by widely accepted management options and dietary guidance from primary-care literature.

Input

Select your symptoms.

Tap each symptom you are currently experiencing. Selection is reflected in the model's binary input vector.

Methodology

Random Forest trained on the real symptom_checker_dataset.xlsx checklist columns across fifteen target conditions. The top-3 output reflects the inherent uncertainty of differential diagnosis from incomplete information.

  • Thirty-symptom vocabulary spanning common presentations.
  • Conditions include respiratory, gastrointestinal, metabolic, urinary, allergic, musculoskeletal, and anxiety presentations.
  • Recommendations are broadly applicable, never prescriptive.

When to seek immediate help.

Sudden chest pain, severe shortness of breath, signs of stroke (facial droop, slurred speech, one-sided weakness), severe abdominal pain, high fever with confusion, or thoughts of self-harm warrant emergency care — not a symptom checker.

What the model cannot see.

A model trained on symptom-disease pairs has no access to your medical history, family history, age, sex, lifestyle, recent travel, or medication list — all of which a clinician weighs by default.
