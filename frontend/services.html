Behind The Platform

Built on principles, not promises.

Modular architecture

Each service is independently deployable, independently testable, and independently upgradable. A failure in one cannot cascade to the others.

Quantitative grounding

Models are selected only after benchmarking five or more candidates against a held-out test set with cross-validation.

Documentation first

Every service ships with a Jupyter notebook documenting the full exploratory analysis, training pipeline, and evaluation.
