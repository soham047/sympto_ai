The Platform

A single interface for five focused healthcare AI tools.

Each module is a self-contained research artefact — exploratory data analysis, model benchmarking, and deployment, fully reproducible from a public dataset. Together they cover the most common day-to-day questions a curious patient might have.

RANDOM FOREST
01 / SERVICE

Blood Report Analyser

Enter the nine standard Complete Blood Count values from a routine pathology report and receive an immediate, calibrated estimate of the most probable haematological category — Normal, Anemia, Infection, Leukopenia, or Thrombocytopenia. Each prediction is paired with class probabilities, an explanatory note, and an evidence-aligned dietary suggestion.

  • Trained on a public CBC dataset of multi-class haematological labels.
  • Ensemble tree model benchmarked against five alternatives.
  • Returns full probability distribution, not just a top guess.

02 / SERVICE

Symptom Checker

Select the symptoms you're experiencing from a curated medical vocabulary. The model returns the three most probable conditions ranked by confidence, accompanied by widely accepted over-the-counter management options and dietary guidance from primary-care literature.

  • Fifteen target conditions spanning respiratory, gastrointestinal, metabolic, urinary, allergic, musculoskeletal, and anxiety presentations.
  • Top-3 ranking reflects the inherent uncertainty of differential diagnosis.
  • Conservative, broadly applicable recommendations — never prescriptive.
Check Symptoms
CNN · TRANSFER LEARNING
03 / SERVICE

Image Diagnosis

Upload a chest radiograph or a dermatological photograph. A Convolutional Neural Network — fine-tuned from a MobileNetV2 backbone pretrained on ImageNet — returns the predicted class and a complete softmax distribution. The pipeline reproduces standard practice in published medical-imaging research.

  • Chest X-ray classifier targeting pneumonia screening.
  • Skin lesion classifier across five common dermatological categories.
  • Two-phase training: frozen head, then partial fine-tuning.
Run Diagnosis
RAG · FAISS
04 / SERVICE

Mental Health Support

A Groq-powered chatbot grounded in curated mental-health references. Retrieval adds helpful background context, while the final reply stays natural and conversational. A crisis-keyword interception layer routes acute queries directly to professional helplines.

  • Reference material is used quietly in the background without showing technical citations.
  • Deterministic safety layer for crisis interception — never overridden by the model.
  • Falls back to local retrieval when Groq is unavailable.
Open Chat
GRADIENT BOOSTING
05 / SERVICE

Lifestyle Planner

Combines a tuned calorie/protein regressor with a planning engine for meals, macros, workouts, steps, hydration, habits, and a four-week schedule. Add your goal, diet pattern, equipment, stress, sleep, and time constraints to generate a realistic lifestyle plan.

  • Five goal modes including fat loss, maintenance, muscle gain, recomposition, and fitness.
  • Workout schedule adapts to equipment, experience, available time, sleep, and stress.
  • Outputs nutrition targets, grocery map, monthly progression, and safety flags.

Principles

Engineering for trust.

Every architectural decision is justified, documented, and reversible. The platform's value is not in any single prediction but in the legibility of how it reaches them.

Transparency

Every model exposes its full probability distribution and underlying methodology.

Privacy

No user account, no persistent storage, no third-party telemetry, no tracking.

Safety

Deterministic interception of distress signals routes acute queries to vetted helplines.

Reproducibility

A single command rebuilds every model artefact from documented public datasets.

Workflow

From input to insight in three deliberate steps.

STEP 01

Choose your service

Select from five purpose-built modules, each addressing a distinct clinical or wellness question with its own dedicated model and interface.

STEP 02

Provide your data

Type numerical values, select symptoms, upload a medical image, or ask a question. Inputs are processed in-memory and never persisted.

STEP 03

Receive grounded output

Each response includes model confidence, supporting context, and a clear disclaimer reminding you to consult a clinician for real decisions.

The goal of medical AI is not to replace the clinician but to compress the time between curiosity and informed action — making preliminary understanding accessible without trivialising the complexity that follows.

— Project authors, technical introduction
