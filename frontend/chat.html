Privacy

Messages are processed in-memory for the duration of the response only. Nothing is logged, stored, or shared with any third party.

How It Answers

Retrieval grounds every response.

Your message is converted to an embedding vector and compared against an index of pre-embedded passages from the curated knowledge base. The three most relevant passages are returned as supporting context and shown alongside the answer.

When It Steps Aside

Acute distress bypasses the model.

A deterministic keyword filter scans every incoming message before reaching the retrieval or generation layer. When phrases indicating acute distress are present, the message is routed directly to vetted helpline information — the model is never consulted.
